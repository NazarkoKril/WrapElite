services

Wrapping auto

Vehicle wrapping with film is a modern way to change the appearance of your car and protect it from scratches and environmental factors. Using special adhesive film, you can create a unique design, preserve the original paintwork, and even use your vehicle for advertising.

order

Paint Protection Film

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a thick yet transparent layer applied to protect your car from potential damages while preserving its original appearance. It is made from polyurethane film with a thickness ranging from 160 to 210 microns, depending on your requirements.

Chip tuning

Chip tuning is a method of increasing the engine's power by unlocking the manufacturer's hidden potential to extend its lifespan. Engine "chipping" can be done by reprogramming the original electronic control unit of the vehicle.

Polishing

Polishing is an essential part of car care, aimed at restoring the shine and quality of the paintwork. This process involves the removal of minor scratches, blemishes, water spots, and other external imperfections that can occur due to vehicle usage.
